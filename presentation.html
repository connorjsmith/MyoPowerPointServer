Introducing the MYO Presentation Aid

Better capabilties than the current MYO presentation tools out there. An enhanced way to deliver your presentation. An exploration into new UX possiblities.

We found the current MYO presentation process a bit cumbersome. Our Presentation Aid provides you with:

  • Redesigned commands that are more intuitive to use
  • Enhanced functionality - Clicking on links, zooming out to slide thumbnails
  • Slide sharing capabilities
  • A seamless presentation experience

The Current Setup

Here's how presenting with MYO currently works:

Move Forward Move Backward Pointer Zoom

Our Gestures

Here's how redefined the gestures:

Previous Slide Next Slide Zoom out to thumbnails Zoom back in Click on link

An Improved User Experience

Above all, our gestures, based on the Nielson's 10 Usability Heuristics, provide an enhanced user experience:

  • Waving right for the next slide and waving left for the previous slide provides for better internal consistency than a double tap with a left wave
  • Opening the fingers to zoom out to thumbnail view and closing the fist to bring it back into full slideshow view provides for external consistency with Apple's multitouch
  • With a more versatile design, our users are granted more flexiblity and freedom
  • Slidesharing provides for a new level of engagement with the content
